Discover the Beauty of Bisibis Falls

Bisibis is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of nature, offering visitors a peaceful and picturesque escape. Here's what you can experience:

  1. Majestic waterfall cascading into crystal-clear pools
  2. Tranquil hiking trails surrounded by lush greenery
  3. Opportunities for swimming and refreshing dips
  4. Picnic areas with breathtaking views of the falls
  5. Guided tours to explore the flora and fauna of the area

How to Get to Bisibis Falls

To reach Bisibis Falls, follow these directions:

  1. From Mauban city proper, take a ride to Brgy. Bao
  2. In Brgy. Bao you will see a signage and direction of Dahoyhoy Falls.
  3. Follow the signs and directions to Dahoyhoy Falls
  4. Follow the designated trail/path to reach Dahoyhoy Falls 15-20mns walk.
