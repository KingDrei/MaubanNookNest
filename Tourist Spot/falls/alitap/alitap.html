ALITAP FALLS

Discover the Beauty of Alitap Falls

Alitap Falls is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of nature, offering visitors a peaceful and picturesque escape. Here's what you can experience:

  1. Majestic waterfall cascading into crystal-clear pools
  2. Tranquil hiking trails surrounded by lush greenery
  3. Opportunities for swimming and refreshing dips
  4. Picnic areas with breathtaking views of the falls
  5. Guided tours to explore the flora and fauna of the area

How to Get to Alitap Falls

To reach Alitap Falls, follow these directions:

  1. From Mauban city proper, take new road in Tapucan towards Brgy.Alitap
  2. Continue on this route until you reach brgy. Alitap and signage.
  3. Follow the signs and directions to Alitap Falls parking area.
  4. Once parked, follow the designated trail/path to reach Alitap Falls 15-20mns walk.
