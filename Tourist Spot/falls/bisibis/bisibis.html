BISIBIS FALLS

Discover the Beauty of Bisibis Falls

Alitap Falls is a hidden gem nestled in the heart of nature, offering visitors a peaceful and picturesque escape. Here's what you can experience:

  1. Majestic waterfall cascading into crystal-clear pools
  2. Opportunities for swimming and refreshing dips
  3. Picnic areas with breathtaking views of the falls
  4. Guided tours to explore the flora and fauna of the area

Plan Your Visit

Plan your trip to Bisibis Falls and immerse yourself in nature's beauty. Don't forget to check out our services and amenities:

  1. Renting Boat: 3,000 roundtrip
  2. Environmental Fee: 10
  3. Entrance Fee: Free

Whether you're a nature enthusiast, adventure seeker, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, Bisibis Falls has something for everyone.

How to Get to Bisibis Falls

To reach bisibis Falls, follow these directions:

  1. From Mauban city proper, take to Mauban Tourism Port in brgy. Daungan
  2. Rent a boat to reach Brgy. Cagsiay 3
  3. After 3-4hrs boat ride, you will see a breathtaking view of falls
