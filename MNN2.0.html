TARA NA SA MAUBAN!

WHY MAUBAN?

Mauban is a charming town located in Quezon province, Philippines. Nestled along the coast of Tayabas Bay, Mauban is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.

The town boasts picturesque beaches, lush forests, and breathtaking waterfalls, making it a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Visitors can explore Cagbalete Island, often dubbed as the "Boracay of the Pacific," with its pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Mauban is also home to vibrant festivals celebrating local traditions and flavorsome cuisine showcasing fresh seafood and native delicacies. The warmth and friendliness of the locals add to the allure of this hidden gem in Quezon province.

DESTINATIONS

CAGBALETE ISLAND

Embark on a journey to Cagbalete Island, known as the 'Boracay of the Pacific.' Immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, offering an unparalleled tropical paradise experience.

CAGSIAY BEACHES

Discover the enchanting allure of Cagsiay Beaches, where over 20 beach resorts await you. Indulge in romantic sunsets and captivating seascapes, making it a perfect escape for couples and beach lovers.

FALLS

Experience the natural wonders of Mauban Falls, boasting five magnificent waterfalls amidst lush greenery. Dive into nature's embrace and rejuvenate your senses in this serene haven for nature enthusiasts.

POOL AND RESORTS

Dive into relaxation and luxury at our exquisite Pool and Resorts, offering a perfect blend of comfort and leisure

LOCAL CULTURE AND HERITAGE
